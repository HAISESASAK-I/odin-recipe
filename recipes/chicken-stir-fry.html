Chicken stir fry

Description:

Elevate your weeknight dinners with our Chicken Stir-Fry, a quick and flavorful dish that brings together the best of textures and tastes. Tender chicken strips dance in a wok with a colorful medley of crisp vegetables, creating a vibrant canvas of freshness.

The magic happens in the sizzling stir-fry sauce, where soy sauce, ginger, and garlic unite to infuse every ingredient with savory perfection. This stir-fry is not just a meal; it's a culinary symphony that's as easy to make as it is delicious.

With its dynamic combination of protein and veggies, our Chicken Stir-Fry promises a delightful crunch in every bite, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a fast, healthy, and utterly satisfying dinner option.

Ingredients:

    Chicken:
  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, thinly sliced.
    • Vegetables:
  • A colorful mix, such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas, sliced or chopped.
    • Soy Sauce:
  • For the savory base of the stir-fry.
    • Ginger:
  • Freshly grated for a burst of flavor.
    • Garlic:
  • Minced to add aromatic depth.
    • Sesame Oil:
  • For a nutty undertone.
    • Vegetable Oil:
  • For stir-frying.
    • Cornstarch:
  • To coat the chicken and thicken the sauce.
    • Sesame Seeds:
  • Optional, for garnish.
    • Green Onions:
  • Sliced, for a fresh finish.

Feel free to adjust the quantities based on your preferences, and enjoy the delicious Chicken Stir-Fry!

Steps:

    Prepare Ingredients:

  1. Slice the chicken thinly and chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.

    2. Marinate Chicken:

  2. In a bowl, mix the sliced chicken with soy sauce and a tablespoon of cornstarch. Let it marinate for 15-20 minutes.

    3. Prepare Stir-Fry Sauce:

  3. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, grated ginger, and minced garlic to create the stir-fry sauce.

    4. Heat the Wok or Pan:

  4. Place a wok or a large skillet over high heat. Add vegetable oil and a splash of sesame oil for flavor.

    5. Stir-Fry Chicken:

  5. Add the marinated chicken to the hot wok. Stir-fry until the chicken is cooked through and slightly browned. Remove the chicken from the wok and set it aside.

    6. Stir-Fry Vegetables:

  6. In the same wok, add more oil if needed. Stir-fry the chopped vegetables until they are slightly tender but still crisp.

    7. Combine Chicken and Vegetables:

  7. Add the cooked chicken back to the wok with the vegetables.

    8. Add Stir-Fry Sauce:

  8. Pour the prepared stir-fry sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Toss everything together to coat evenly.

    9. Thicken the Sauce:

  9. If you desire a thicker sauce, mix a little cornstarch with water and add it to the wok. Stir until the sauce thickens.

    10. Garnish and Serve:

  10. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions. Serve the Chicken Stir-Fry hot over rice or noodles.
